About Us

Swabalambi Odisha is affiliated to a Government of India funded Incubation centre, which by itself is an institution. We are also affiliated to privately owned and operated Incubation centres, in Odisha. Vital tools like infrastructure, technology, mentors, fund opportunities are all readily available under one roof.

Our Leadership Team

Dr. Rajesh Kumar

Executive Director

Leading our organization with over 15 years of experience in disability rights and community development.

Priya Sharma

Program Coordinator

Specialized in inclusive education and skill development programs for persons with disabilities.

Amit Patel

Community Outreach Manager

Dedicated to building partnerships and raising awareness about disability inclusion.

Our Services

Skill Development

Providing vocational training and skill development programs for self-reliance.

Education Support

Assisting with inclusive education and learning resources for all ages.

Community Outreach

Engaging with communities to raise awareness and promote inclusion.
